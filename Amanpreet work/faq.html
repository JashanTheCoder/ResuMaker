Frequently Asked Questions

Will my Premium account renew automatically?

No, our pricing model is not subscription based. Therefore, you will not be charged automatically without your consent.

Will my information be saved after the Premium period expires?

All the information will be saved in your account, even when the Premium period expires.

Are there any hidden costs?

There are no hidden costs. After selecting the period, the total price that you will be charged can be seen before introducing any payment details.

Can I customize the templates?

Absolutely! Our templates are highly customizable. You can change fonts, colors, and layouts to match your personal style or the industry standard.

What information should I include in my resume?

Your resume should feature your contact information, a strong professional summary, detailed work experience with accomplishments, education background, relevant skills, and certifications.

How can I contact support if I have issues?

If you encounter any problems or have questions, please contact our support team through the website's contact form or email us at biobanao.com.

How do I download my resume?

After finishing your resume, click the 'Download' button and choose the format you want: PDF, Word, or others.

Yes, we prioritize data security and have implemented various encryption measures to keep your information safe.
