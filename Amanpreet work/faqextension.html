Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section! Here, you'll find answers to some of the most commonly asked questions about my services, processes, and web development in general. If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out through the contact page.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my Premium account renew automatically?

No, our pricing model is not subscription based. Therefore, you will not be charged automatically without your consent.

Will my information be saved after the Premium period expires?

All the information will be saved in your account, even when the Premium period expires.

Are there any hidden costs?

There are no hidden costs. After selecting the period, the total price that you will be charged can be seen before introducing any payment details. (This excludes the charges required by the bank transfer.)

Can I customize the templates?

Absolutely! Our templates are highly customizable. You can change fonts, colors, and layouts to match your personal style or the industry standard. This flexibility allows you to create a resume that stands out while maintaining professionalism

What information should I include in my resume ?

Your resume should feature your contact information, a strong professional summary, detailed work experience with accomplishments, education background, relevant skills, and certifications. Tailor each section to align with the job description and highlight experiences that demonstrate your qualifications

How can I contact support if I have issues?

If you encounter any problems or have questions, please contact our support team through the website's contact form or email us at biobanao.com. Our team is dedicated to providing prompt assistance to ensure a smooth experience..

If you have any other questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm here to help!
